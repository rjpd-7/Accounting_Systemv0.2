Chart of Accounts Basic Information

Chart of accounts : A chart of accounts (COA) is an index of all of the financial accounts in a company's general ledger. In short, it is an organizational tool that lists by category and line item all of the financial transactions that a company conducted during a specific accounting period.


Different Account Types : Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Revenue, Expenses.
